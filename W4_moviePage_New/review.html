REVIEW


Tristen Jussaume August 7th, 2023

Revenge of the Sith is my favorite Star Wars movie because it gives a lot of backstory to episodes 4-6 like how Anakin becomes Darth Vader, what happened to all the Jedi, why Yoda went into hiding, and SOOOOO much more. Additionally I think Revenge of the Sith is the most well balanced out of all the movies in terms of graphics, story arc, action, and drama. For a movie that is almost 20 years old now I think it still holds up pretty well.
